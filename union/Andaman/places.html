Travel Andaman & Nicobar

Tropical Bliss & Untouched Beauty



Port Blair

Cellular Jail

Historic colonial-era prison known for its light & sound show on Indian freedom struggle.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Closed: Mondays

Corbyn's Cove Beach

Close to Port Blair, ideal for jet skiing and relaxing beach walks.

Best Time: Nov – Apr

Note: Open daily

Samudrika Museum

Marine museum showcasing island biodiversity, tribal culture, and history.

Best Time: Year-round

Closed: Mondays



Havelock Island

Radhanagar Beach

Ranked among Asia’s best beaches for its turquoise waters and sunset views.

Best Time: Oct – May

Note: Lifeguards available

Elephant Beach

Famous for water sports like snorkeling, glass boat rides, and coral watching.

Best Time: Oct – May

Note: Boat ride required

Kalapathar Beach

Quiet, scenic beach lined with black rocks and golden sands.

Best Time: Early mornings & sunset

Note: No water sports here



Neil Island

Natural Bridge

Also known as Howrah Bridge, it’s a natural rock formation shaped by waves.

Best Time: Low tide (morning)

Note: Slippery rocks

Bharatpur Beach

Best beach on Neil Island for swimming and coral snorkeling.

Best Time: Oct – Apr

Note: Family-friendly
