Staying Options in Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Hotels

Andaman offers serene beachfront hotels and eco-resorts in places like Port Blair, Havelock (Swaraj Dweep), and Neil Island.

SeaShell Hotel, Port Blair

Luxury sea-facing hotel with rooftop dining and modern comfort.

Barefoot Resort, Havelock

Eco-luxury cottages nestled in lush greenery near Radhanagar Beach.

Silver Sand Beach Resort, Neil Island

Beautiful beachfront location with tropical vibes and calm waters.

Guest Houses

Guest houses provide homely stays and local hospitality at affordable prices, ideal for couples and solo travelers.

Aashiaanaa Residency Inn, Port Blair

Comfortable rooms and helpful staff near Aberdeen Bazaar.

Pearl Park Guest House, Neil Island

Quiet stay with beach access and simple comforts.

Tango Waves Guest House, Havelock

Budget stay near ferry terminal with cozy rooms and AC.

Homestays

Experience island life by staying with locals, enjoying home-cooked seafood and learning about tribal and fishing culture.

Island Breeze Homestay, Port Blair

Warm hosts, local cuisine, and sea views in a peaceful neighborhood.

Coral View Homestay, Neil Island

Rustic charm with beach proximity and homey vibe.

Ocean Heart Homestay, Havelock

Friendly family-run place surrounded by coconut groves.

Budget Lodges

Perfect for backpackers and adventure lovers, these budget lodges offer basic comforts and proximity to beaches and markets.

Dolphin Lodge, Port Blair

Budget rooms with travel desk and easy access to Cellular Jail.

Beach Camp Lodge, Havelock

Simple bamboo huts just steps away from the beach.

Island Stay Lodge, Neil Island

Affordable stay near the jetty and Laxmanpur Beach.
