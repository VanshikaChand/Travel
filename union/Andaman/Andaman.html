Welcome to Andaman & Nicobar Islands



Tropical Serenity and Azure Waters Await



Beaches & Sunsets

Relax at Radhanagar Beach, Kalapathar, and Laxmanpur. Powder-white sands and golden sunsets make the Andaman shoreline unforgettable.

Coral Reefs & Diving

Explore the underwater world at Havelock, Neil, and North Bay with scuba diving, snorkeling, and glass-bottom boat rides in vibrant coral gardens.

Cellular Jail

Witness the colonial-era “Kala Pani” prison and its light-and-sound show — a stirring tribute to India’s freedom fighters and the island’s turbulent history.

Tribal Reserves

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands are home to indigenous tribes like the Jarwa and Nicobarese. While protected, their presence enriches the cultural mosaic.

Limestone Caves

Explore the stunning limestone caves of Baratang Island by boat — an adventure through mangroves, mud volcanoes, and ancient stone formations.

Island Hopping

From Port Blair to Neil Island, each destination offers unique charm — colonial relics, local bazaars, pristine waters, and vibrant island life await your discovery.
