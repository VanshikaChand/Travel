Staying Options in Dadra and Nagar Haveli, Daman and Diu

Hotels

From sea-view resorts in Diu to heritage stays in Daman and business hotels in Silvassa, this region offers diverse hotel experiences.

The Deltin Hotel, Daman

Lavish hotel with pool, spa, and casino close to Devka Beach.

Radhika Beach Resort, Diu

Popular beachfront resort with lush gardens and sea-view rooms.

Lords Resort, Silvassa

Peaceful resort with modern amenities near Vanganga Lake Garden.

Guest Houses

Affordable and clean, guest houses in Daman, Diu, and Silvassa offer a comfortable experience for families and solo tourists.

Blue Lagoon Guest House, Daman

Simple, friendly guest house within walking distance to Jampore Beach.

Sea Shell Guest House, Diu

Well-rated stay near Diu Fort with sea-view terrace rooms.

Hill View Guest House, Silvassa

Budget option close to Tribal Museum and Deer Park.

Homestays

For a more local experience, choose homestays that reflect the Portuguese heritage, Goan flavors, and tribal influences of the region.

Casa de Diu Homestay

Traditional Portuguese home stay with garden courtyard and local cuisine.

Coastal Comfort Homestay, Daman

Family-run property near the beach with warm hospitality and fresh meals.

Tribal Nest, Silvassa

Ethnic-style homestay showcasing tribal crafts, food, and culture.

Budget Lodges

Budget travelers can find neat and cheap lodges near bus stands, markets, and coastal spots across the union territory.

Sunrise Lodge, Diu

Small lodge with clean rooms and helpful staff near Naida Caves.

Devka Inn Lodge, Daman

Economical lodge just minutes away from Devka Beach and restaurants.

Budget Stay Lodge, Silvassa

Low-cost rooms with basic facilities in the city center.
