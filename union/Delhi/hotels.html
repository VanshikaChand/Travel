Staying Options in Delhi

Hotels

Delhi offers a range of accommodation options from luxurious 5-star hotels to modern boutique stays in areas like Connaught Place, Aerocity, and South Delhi.

Taj Palace, Delhi

Elegant 5-star hotel with lush gardens and fine dining in Chanakyapuri.

The Lalit, Connaught Place

Luxury hotel in central Delhi with spa and city views.

Roseate House, Aerocity

Contemporary hotel near the airport with modern amenities.

Guest Houses

Guest houses in Delhi offer affordable and comfortable options, ideal for students, solo travelers, and business visitors.

Asha Guest House, Karol Bagh

Budget-friendly stay with easy metro access.

Comfort Inn, Paharganj

Basic amenities in a bustling area close to railway station.

Sukhdev Guest House, South Delhi

Peaceful surroundings with home-style comfort.

Homestays

Delhi's homestays offer a personal experience with local families, ideal for cultural immersion in areas like Hauz Khas, Nizamuddin, and Shahpur Jat.

Hauz Khas Heritage Stay

Charming rooms near historic sites and cafés.

Nizamuddin Homestay

Live near Sufi shrines with traditional food experiences.

Shahpur Jat Boutique Homestay

Trendy and creative stay near urban art hubs.

Budget Lodges

Travelers on a budget can find several clean and cheap lodges across the city, especially around Paharganj and Old Delhi.

City Star Lodge, Paharganj

Close to New Delhi Railway Station, with basic facilities.

Red Fort Lodge, Old Delhi

Budget-friendly and located near historic attractions.

Backpackers Inn, Saket

Clean, youthful atmosphere for solo travelers and students.
