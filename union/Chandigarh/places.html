Travel Chandigarh

Where Urban Meets Nature



Nature & Leisure

Rock Garden

A sculpture garden made entirely from industrial and urban waste.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Note: Open daily

Sukhna Lake

Man-made reservoir ideal for boating, walking, and serene sunsets.

Best Time: Morning or Sunset

Note: Open till 8 PM

Zakir Rose Garden

Asia’s largest rose garden with over 1,600 species of roses and fountains.

Best Time: Feb – Mar (Rose Festival)

Note: Open daily



Culture & Heritage

Government Museum & Art Gallery

Rich collection of Gandhara sculptures, Pahari miniatures, and Le Corbusier artifacts.

Best Time: Year-round

Closed: Mondays

Le Corbusier Centre

Dedicated to the Swiss-French architect who designed Chandigarh.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Note: Free entry

Capitol Complex

UNESCO World Heritage Site housing Secretariat, High Court & Assembly.

Best Time: Oct – Feb

Note: Prior permission required



Entertainment & Shopping

Elante Mall

One of the largest malls in North India with brands, cinema, food court.

Best Time: Weekdays for less crowd

Note: Open daily

Sector 17 Plaza

Open-air market with fountains, shopping arcades, and events.

Best Time: Evening for light shows

Note: Family-friendly
