Staying Options in Chandigarh

Hotels

Chandigarh offers a variety of upscale and modern hotels with scenic views, excellent service, and central locations near Sector 17, IT Park, and Sukhna Lake.

JW Marriott, Chandigarh

Luxury 5-star hotel with rooftop pool and fine dining in Sector 35.

Taj Chandigarh

Modern hotel near Sector 17 with spacious rooms and premium services.

The LaLiT, Chandigarh

Elegant ambiance with lush lawns and wellness center in Rajiv Gandhi IT Park.

Guest Houses

Chandigarh's guest houses are known for cleanliness, calm surroundings, and easy accessibility to gardens and markets.

Sector 22 Guest House

Peaceful residential stay close to the bus stand and shopping areas.

Green Valley Guest House

Budget guest house in Sector 49 with good amenities and parking.

Lakeview Residency

Quiet guest house near Sukhna Lake with clean and airy rooms.

Homestays

Enjoy a blend of hospitality and local experiences through homestays in peaceful neighborhoods of Chandigarh.

Modern Nest Homestay

Warm hosts, home-cooked meals, and close to Elante Mall.

Sector 10 Urban Homestay

Charming stay with garden views and minimalist decor.

Rose Garden Villa

Homely space near the famous Zakir Rose Garden with cozy rooms.

Budget Lodges

Perfect for travelers seeking low-cost accommodation with basic comforts, Chandigarh’s lodges are located in convenient sectors and transport hubs.

City Lodge, Sector 45

Basic but clean rooms with easy access to ISBT.

Budget Inn, Mani Majra

Affordable and close to Panchkula border and IT Park.

Travelers Stay, Sector 43

Simple accommodation for short stays with local eateries nearby.
