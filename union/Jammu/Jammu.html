Welcome to Jammu & Kashmir



Paradise on Earth



Heavenly Landscapes

From the lush meadows of Gulmarg and Pahalgam to the snowy peaks of Sonamarg, every view in Jammu & Kashmir is a living postcard.

Dal Lake & Shikara Rides

Float through calm waters in Srinagar on colorful shikaras. Stay in traditional houseboats and enjoy stunning Himalayan reflections.

Mughal Gardens

Walk through Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi — terraced gardens that bloom with tulips, fountains, and ancient architecture.

Adventure Sports

Try skiing in Gulmarg, white water rafting in Lidder, trekking in the Himalayas, or paragliding over valleys for adrenaline-filled experiences.

Spiritual Sites

Visit Vaishno Devi Temple in Jammu and the serene Hazratbal Shrine or Shankaracharya Temple in Srinagar — spiritual journeys in majestic settings.

Kashmiri Culture

Admire handcrafted Pashmina shawls, intricate papier-mâché art, and savor Wazwan cuisine — a royal feast rich in spices and tradition.
