Explore Jammu & Kashmir

Paradise on Earth



Srinagar

Dal Lake

Iconic lake with houseboats and shikara rides, surrounded by the Zabarwan hills.

Best Time: Apr – Oct

Note: Famous for floating market

Mughal Gardens

Chashme Shahi, Nishat Bagh, and Shalimar Bagh showcasing Persian-style beauty.

Best Time: Spring & Summer

Note: Open daily

Shankaracharya Temple

Ancient Shiva temple on a hill with panoramic views of Srinagar city.

Best Time: Year-round

Note: Security check required



Gulmarg & Sonmarg

Gulmarg

Popular for skiing, snowboarding, and the famous Gulmarg Gondola ride.

Best Time: Dec – Mar (snow) | May – Sep (green meadows)

Note: Ideal for adventure lovers

Sonmarg

"Meadow of Gold" with glaciers, river rafting, and trekking trails.

Best Time: May – Sep

Note: Often snow-covered till April



Jammu

Vaishno Devi Temple

One of India's most revered pilgrimage sites nestled in the Trikuta Mountains.

Best Time: Mar – Oct

Note: 12-km uphill trek (helicopter & pony options available)

Raghunath Temple

Famous temple complex dedicated to Lord Rama in the heart of Jammu city.

Best Time: Anytime

Note: Security check mandatory
