Staying Options in Jammu and Kashmir

Hotels

From luxurious stays in Srinagar to well-located hotels in Jammu and Gulmarg, the region offers comfort with breathtaking views.

The Lalit Grand Palace, Srinagar

Former royal residence turned into a luxury hotel near Dal Lake.

Hotel Pine Spring, Gulmarg

Warm and comfortable stay with scenic views of snow-capped peaks.

Radisson Blu, Jammu

Modern amenities and premium service in the heart of Jammu city.

Guest Houses

Affordable guest houses provide peaceful stays with local hospitality and mountain charm.

Kashmir Guest House, Srinagar

Charming houseboat-style stay with stunning lake views.

Alpine Guest House, Pahalgam

Friendly staff, pine surroundings, and traditional wooden decor.

Vaishno Residency, Katra

Budget guest house near the base of Vaishno Devi trek route.

Homestays

Experience authentic Kashmiri culture with homestays offering home-cooked meals, warmth, and local stories.

Snow View Homestay, Srinagar

Traditional Kashmiri home with a garden and warm hosts.

Mountain Breeze Homestay, Gulmarg

Charming wooden home with views of meadows and peaks.

Heritage Homestay, Jammu

Blend of tradition and comfort in a local family setting.

Budget Lodges

For budget-conscious travelers, basic lodges in all major towns offer economical and comfortable accommodations.

New Light Lodge, Srinagar

Popular among backpackers, close to Shankaracharya Temple.

Green Valley Lodge, Pahalgam

Affordable lodge surrounded by pine trees and nature.

Hilltop Lodge, Katra

Simple and clean stay for pilgrims visiting Vaishno Devi.
