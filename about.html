About Us

Know more about our journey and mission

Who We Are

Welcome to Explore India — a student-built travel guide created with passion and dedication to showcase the beauty, culture, and hidden gems of Indian states. Whether you're a local explorer or a traveler from afar, our platform provides insights, tips, and inspiration for your next journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make travel easier, smarter, and more meaningful. We aim to:

  • Provide well-organized travel information about Indian states.
  • Highlight culture, food, staying options, and local experiences.
  • Encourage responsible and informed travel practices.

Meet the Creator

Hi! I'm Vanshika Chand, a student passionate about web development, travel, and cultural exploration. This project was designed as part of my learning journey, combining research, design, and coding into one vibrant travel platform.
