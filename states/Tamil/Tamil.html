Welcome to Tamil Nadu



Explore the Cultural Heart of South India



Majestic Temples

Marvel at the architectural brilliance of temples like Meenakshi Amman Temple, Brihadeeswarar Temple, and Rameswaram, showcasing Dravidian art and devotion.

Vibrant Festivals

Experience Pongal, Tamil New Year, and temple festivals filled with colorful rituals, music, dance, and traditional cuisine.

Beautiful Coastlines

Relax on the golden beaches of Marina, Mahabalipuram, and Kanyakumari where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet.

Classical Arts & Dance

Discover Bharatanatyam performances and Carnatic music concerts that have thrived here for centuries.
