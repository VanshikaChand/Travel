Welcome to Chhattisgarh



A Tapestry of Waterfalls, Forests, and Tribes



Dense Forests & Wildlife

Explore the abundant forests of Kanger Valley and Barnawapara, home to diverse flora and fauna including leopards, tigers, and many bird species.

Spectacular Waterfalls

Visit the mesmerizing Chitrakote Falls, known as the "Niagara of India," along with Tirathgarh and Amrit Dhara waterfalls for breathtaking natural beauty.

Rich Tribal Culture

Experience the vibrant traditions, folk dances like Panthi and Raut Nacha, and handicrafts of over 40 tribal communities living harmoniously in Chhattisgarh.

Ancient Temples

Discover historic temples like the Bhoramdeo Temple and Danteshwari Temple, reflecting intricate architecture and spiritual significance.

Adventure & Nature Trails

Trek through the lush landscapes of Achanakmar Wildlife Sanctuary or enjoy boating at the serene Hasdeo River.

Festivals & Fairs

Join in the celebrations of Bastar Dussehra, Goncha Festival, and other tribal fairs that highlight the cultural richness and joyous spirit of Chhattisgarh.
