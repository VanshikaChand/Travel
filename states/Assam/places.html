Travel Assam

Land of Tea Gardens, Rhinos & River Islands



Kamrup District

Kamakhya Temple

One of the oldest and most revered Shakti Peethas in India.

Best Time: October – April

Guwahati

Gateway to the Northeast, bustling city on the banks of Brahmaputra.

Highlight: River cruises, Umananda Island

Assam State Zoo

Largest zoo in Northeast India with rare species including the one-horned rhinoceros.

Note: Closed on Fridays



Golaghat District

Kaziranga National Park

UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to the one-horned rhinoceros.

Best Time: November – April

Hollongapar Gibbon Sanctuary

Only sanctuary in India dedicated to the hoolock gibbons.

Best Time: October – March



Majuli District

Majuli Island

World's largest river island on the Brahmaputra River, known for Vaishnavite culture.

Best Time: October – March



Dibrugarh District

Tea Gardens

Famous for sprawling tea estates producing some of the finest tea in the world.

Highlight: Tea factory visits & tastings

Dibru-Saikhowa National Park

Known for feral horses, migratory birds, and riverine biodiversity.

Best Time: November – April
