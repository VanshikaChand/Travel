Welcome to Punjab



The Land of Five Rivers & Vibrant Spirit



Golden Temple, Amritsar

Visit the iconic Harmandir Sahib, a stunning golden shrine and spiritual center of Sikhism.

Wagah Border Ceremony

Experience the thrilling daily flag-lowering ceremony between India and Pakistan, full of patriotism and energy.

Sheesh Mahal, Patiala

Explore the palace of mirrors with exquisite architecture and royal history.

Baisakhi Festival

Join the grand harvest festival celebrations with vibrant folk dances like Bhangra and Gidda.

Traditional Punjabi Cuisine

Savor hearty dishes like Makki di Roti, Sarson da Saag, Butter Chicken, and Amritsari Kulcha.

Lohri Festival

Celebrate the winter harvest with bonfires, folk songs, and dances marking new beginnings.
