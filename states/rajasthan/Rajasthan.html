Welcome to Rajasthan

The Land of Kings & Royal Grandeur

Jaipur - The Pink City

Famous for Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and its vibrant bazaars full of colors and handicrafts.

Thar Desert

Enjoy camel rides, desert safaris, and golden sunsets across the dunes of Jaisalmer.

Royal Palaces

Explore majestic palaces and forts in Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Bikaner echoing the Rajput legacy.

Traditional Dance & Music

Experience Ghoomar, Kalbeliya, and soulful Rajasthani folk music.

Desert Festival

A vibrant celebration in Jaisalmer with folk performances, camel races, and fireworks.

Delicious Cuisine

Relish dishes like Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi, Laal Maas, and Ghewar.
