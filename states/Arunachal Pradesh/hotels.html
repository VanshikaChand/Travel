Staying Options in Arunachal Pradesh

Hotels

From capital city Itanagar to tourist hubs like Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh offers comfortable hotels with beautiful views and good hospitality.


Hotel Donyi Polo, Itanagar

Well-maintained hotel with modern amenities and convenient location.


Tawang Residency, Tawang

Comfortable stay with scenic mountain views near Tawang Monastery.


Hotel Nyukmad, Ziro

Good quality hotel close to Ziro’s scenic valley and local markets.

Eco Resorts

Eco resorts in Arunachal Pradesh provide a nature-friendly experience, blending with the beautiful surroundings of forests and hills.

Arunachal Eco Resort, Dirang

Experience eco-friendly living with modern comforts near hot springs.

Ziro Eco Retreat, Ziro Valley

Peaceful eco-cottages surrounded by pine forests and rice fields.

Sela Pass Eco Camp, Sela

Unique eco-tents with breathtaking views at high altitude.

Guest Houses

Budget-friendly guest houses with warm hospitality are common in towns and near popular tourist destinations.

Tawang Guest House

Affordable stay close to Tawang market and sightseeing spots.

Itanagar Guest Lodge

Basic accommodation with friendly service in the state capital.

Ziro Valley Guest House

Cozy rooms offering a local experience in the scenic Ziro Valley.

Homestays

Homestays provide a unique opportunity to live with local tribes and experience Arunachal Pradesh’s rich cultural heritage.

Apatani Tribal Homestay, Ziro

Immerse yourself in the traditional Apatani lifestyle and cuisine.

Monpa Village Homestay, Tawang

Experience Monpa tribal culture with warm local hospitality.

Adi Tribe Homestay, Pasighat

Stay with the Adi tribe and learn about their customs and festivals.
