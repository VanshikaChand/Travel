Welcome to Uttar Pradesh



Land of Heritage and Spirituality



Taj Mahal

Witness the breathtaking beauty of the Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbol of eternal love.

Varanasi Ghats

Experience the spiritual vibe of Varanasi’s ghats along the sacred Ganges River, the oldest living city in the world.

Fatehpur Sikri

Explore Fatehpur Sikri, the ancient Mughal capital with magnificent palaces, forts, and mosques.

Lucknow Nawabi Culture

Dive into the rich Nawabi culture of Lucknow, famous for its cuisine, music, and traditional dance forms.

Kumbh Mela

Witness the world’s largest religious gathering, Kumbh Mela, held on the banks of the Ganges in Allahabad (Prayagraj).

Awadhi Cuisine

Savor the flavors of Awadhi cuisine including kebabs, biryanis, and rich Mughlai dishes.
