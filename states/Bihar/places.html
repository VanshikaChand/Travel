Travel Bihar

Land of Heritage, Culture & Spirituality



Patna

Golghar

An iconic granary built in 1786 with panoramic views of Patna city.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Note: Open daily

Takht Sri Patna Sahib

A major Sikh pilgrimage site, birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh Ji.

Best Time: All year

Note: Open daily

Bihar Museum

Modern museum showcasing the rich history and culture of Bihar.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Closed: Mondays



Bodh Gaya

Mahabodhi Temple

UNESCO World Heritage Site where Buddha attained enlightenment.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Note: Open daily

Great Buddha Statue

A massive 80-foot high statue symbolizing peace and enlightenment.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Note: Open daily

Thai Monastery

Peaceful monastery with beautiful Thai architecture and gardens.

Best Time: Oct – Mar

Note: Open daily



Nalanda

Nalanda University Ruins

Ancient center of learning and UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Best Time: Oct – Feb

Note: Open daily

Nalanda Archaeological Museum

Displays artifacts excavated from the ancient university site.

Best Time: Oct – Feb

Closed: Mondays

Hieun Tsang Memorial Hall

Dedicated to the famous Chinese traveler who studied at Nalanda.

Best Time: Oct – Feb

Note: Open daily
