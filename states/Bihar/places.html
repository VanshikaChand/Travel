Travel Bihar

Land of Enlightenment, Heritage & History



Patna District

Golghar

A granary built by the British in 1786, now a prominent tourist spot in Patna.

Highlight: Panoramic view of Patna city

Patna Museum

Houses rare artifacts from ancient India, including the Didarganj Yakshi.

Note: Closed on Mondays



Gaya District

Mahabodhi Temple

UNESCO World Heritage Site where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.

Best Time: October – March

Vishnupad Temple

Ancient Hindu temple believed to have Lord Vishnu’s footprints.

Note: Sacred to both locals and pilgrims



Nalanda District

Nalanda University Ruins

Ruins of the ancient university, once a global center of learning.

Best Time: October – February



Vaishali District

Ashokan Pillar

Built by Emperor Ashoka, this pillar marks the spot of Lord Buddha’s last sermon.

Significance: Symbol of peace & Dharma
