Welcome to Bihar



Cradle of Ancient Civilizations and Enlightenment



Ancient Heritage

Explore the ancient ruins of Nalanda University, one of the world’s oldest universities, and the historic city of Rajgir, significant in Buddhist history.

Spiritual Centers

Visit Bodh Gaya, where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment, and other spiritual landmarks that attract pilgrims worldwide.

Madhubani Art

Discover the vibrant and intricate folk art of Madhubani, characterized by colorful patterns and themes inspired by nature and mythology.

The Ganges River

Experience the spiritual vibe along the Ganges in Patna and Munger, with beautiful ghats and vibrant riverfront life.

Festivals & Culture

Celebrate Chhath Puja, Sonepur Mela, and other cultural events that showcase the deep traditions and community spirit of Bihar.

Architectural Wonders

Explore the historic ruins of Vikramshila University, ancient temples, and forts that tell stories of Bihar’s rich past.
