Welcome to Karnataka



A Harmony of Heritage, Hills, and Hitech



Royal Heritage

Walk through the majestic Mysore Palace, and explore the royal legacy of Tipu Sultan and Wodeyars in Mysuru and Srirangapatna.

Ancient Temples & Architecture

Marvel at intricate carvings in Hampi, Belur, Halebidu, and Badami — UNESCO heritage sites and ancient temple towns of Karnataka.

Western Ghats & Wildlife

Venture into the lush Western Ghats with treks in Coorg, Chikmagalur and safaris in Bandipur and Nagarhole wildlife sanctuaries.

Spiritual Journeys

Visit Dharmasthala, Sringeri, and Udupi for divine peace and serene temples that reflect deep-rooted faith and devotion.

Modern Bengaluru

Experience the vibrant tech capital, garden parks like Lalbagh & Cubbon, and nightlife of India’s Silicon Valley – Bengaluru.

Beaches & Coffee

Unwind at Gokarna’s serene beaches or sip freshly brewed coffee in Chikmagalur’s estates — a perfect Karnataka escape.
