Welcome to Odisha



The Soul of Eastern India



Konark Sun Temple

Marvel at the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the 13th-century Sun Temple, famous for its intricate stone carvings and chariot design.

Puri Beach & Jagannath Temple

Enjoy the golden sands of Puri Beach and visit the revered Jagannath Temple, a major pilgrimage spot for Hindus.

Chilika Lake

Explore Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon, home to migratory birds, dolphins, and stunning natural scenery.

Odissi Dance & Culture

Witness the graceful Odissi classical dance form and immerse yourself in Odisha's rich cultural heritage.

Handicrafts & Textiles

Shop for exquisite Pattachitra paintings, applique work, and handwoven textiles that showcase Odisha’s craftsmanship.

Local Cuisine

Delight in authentic Odia dishes like Dalma, Pakhala Bhata, and seafood specialties rich in spices and flavor.
