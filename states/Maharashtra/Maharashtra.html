Welcome to Maharashtra



Discover the Heart of India's Diversity



Mumbai - The City of Dreams

Explore the bustling metropolis, Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and vibrant street life, blending history with modernity.

Ajanta & Ellora Caves

Marvel at ancient rock-cut Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain cave temples adorned with exquisite murals and sculptures.

Beaches & Hill Stations

Relax at the serene beaches of Alibaug, Ganpatipule, and visit the scenic hill stations like Lonavala and Mahabaleshwar.

Cultural Festivals

Celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi with grand processions and folk dances that showcase Maharashtra’s rich traditions.

Historic Forts

Discover the legacy of Maratha Empire by visiting forts like Raigad, Shivneri, and Sindhudurg.

Cuisine & Handicrafts

Savor delicious Maharashtrian dishes like Vada Pav, Misal Pav, and explore Warli art and Paithani silk sarees.
