Welcome to Haryana



The Heart of Heroes and Heritage



Kurukshetra

The legendary battlefield of Mahabharata, Kurukshetra is a sacred site with ghats, temples, and the famous Brahma Sarovar.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for bird watchers, especially in winter when migratory birds arrive. Enjoy nature walks and peaceful surroundings.

Rural Tourism

Experience authentic Haryanvi culture through village stays, traditional food, pottery, and folk performances at farm resorts.

Panipat

One of India's historic battlegrounds. Visit the Panipat Museum and forts to learn about the battles that shaped India.

Rock Garden (Panchkula)

Enjoy art made from recycled materials in a whimsical garden setting, perfect for both adults and children.

Mansa Devi Temple

Located in Panchkula, it is a revered Shakti temple that draws pilgrims from all over, especially during Navratri festivals.
