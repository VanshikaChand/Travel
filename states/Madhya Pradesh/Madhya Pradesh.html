Welcome to Madhya Pradesh



The Heart of Incredible India



Historical Marvels

Explore UNESCO World Heritage Sites like the Khajuraho Temples with their exquisite carvings and the majestic forts of Gwalior and Orchha, echoing the grandeur of India's past.

Wildlife Sanctuaries

Visit Kanha and Bandhavgarh National Parks to see Bengal tigers, Indian leopards, and diverse flora and fauna in their natural habitat. A paradise for wildlife enthusiasts and photographers.

Cultural Festivals

Experience vibrant festivals like the Tansen Music Festival, Khajuraho Dance Festival, and Bhagoria Tribal Festival that showcase Madhya Pradesh’s rich cultural tapestry.

Spiritual Destinations

Visit the spiritual town of Ujjain, famous for the Kumbh Mela and Mahakaleshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India.

Natural Beauty

Enjoy the scenic beauty of the Satpura and Vindhya mountain ranges, waterfalls, and the serene Pachmarhi hill station, the only hill station in Madhya Pradesh.

Handicrafts & Cuisine

Discover Madhya Pradesh’s traditional handicrafts, including Chanderi and Maheshwari sarees. Savor local cuisine that blends rich flavors and spices unique to the region.
