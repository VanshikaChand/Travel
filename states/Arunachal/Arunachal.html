Welcome to Arunachal Pradesh



Land of the Rising Sun and Pristine Peaks



Majestic Monasteries

Home to the iconic Tawang Monastery, Arunachal Pradesh offers a spiritual escape into Himalayan Buddhism, with peaceful prayer chants, intricate architecture, and breathtaking mountain backdrops.

Mesmerizing Landscapes

From the snowy peaks of Sela Pass to the lush valleys of Ziro and Mechuka, the state is a haven for nature lovers. Experience serene lakes, alpine forests, and panoramic Himalayan views.

Tribal Culture

Explore the vibrant tribal heritage of over 20 major indigenous communities. Witness traditional festivals, dances like Ponung and Reh, and handwoven textiles rich in symbolism and color.

Adventure & Trekking

Adventurers can trek to Gorichen Base Camp or scale the Dirang Valley’s trails. River rafting in Siang and camping in the untouched wilderness offer thrill-seekers unforgettable memories.

Exotic Wildlife

Visit Namdapha National Park to spot red pandas, snow leopards, and hornbills. Arunachal’s rich biodiversity and pristine forest reserves are ideal for eco-tourism and wildlife photography.

Cultural Festivals

Celebrate festivals like Losar, Dree, and Mopin that showcase Arunachal's unity in diversity. Enjoy folk music, traditional cuisines, and age-old customs in a joyful, community spirit.
