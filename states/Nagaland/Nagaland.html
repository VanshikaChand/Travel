Welcome to Nagaland



The Land of Festivals & Hills



Hornbill Festival

Experience Nagaland's vibrant Hornbill Festival, a spectacular showcase of tribal dances, crafts, music, and traditions held every December.

Majestic Hills & Valleys

Explore the beautiful landscapes of Kohima and Dimapur, with lush hills, serene valleys, and scenic trekking paths.

Tribal Heritage

Discover the rich traditions of Nagaland’s diverse tribes, each with unique attire, crafts, and festivals that reflect their ancient roots.

Traditional Crafts

Shop exquisite handwoven shawls, beadwork, and wood carvings that tell the story of Naga artistry and craftsmanship.

War Cemeteries & History

Visit the Kohima War Cemetery and learn about Nagaland’s role in World War II, remembering the brave soldiers and rich history.

Local Cuisine

Relish authentic Naga dishes like smoked pork with bamboo shoot, spicy chutneys, and fresh river fish specialties.
