Welcome to Andhra Pradesh



The Essence of Spirituality and Sunrise



Historical Temples

Andhra Pradesh boasts magnificent temples like Tirupati Balaji and the ancient Lepakshi Temple, blending stunning architecture with deep spiritual significance.

Scenic Beaches

Relax on the golden sands of beaches such as Rishikonda and Rushikonda near Visakhapatnam, where you can enjoy sunbathing, water sports, and stunning sunsets over the Bay of Bengal.

Vibrant Festivals

Experience grand celebrations like Ugadi, Sankranti, and the colorful Kuchipudi dance festivals that highlight the state's rich cultural heritage.

Natural Wonders & Hills

Visit the lush Araku Valley, famous for coffee plantations and tribal culture, or the scenic hills of Horsley Hills offering cool weather and panoramic views.

Rich Cuisine

Indulge in Andhra Pradesh’s spicy and flavorful cuisine, known for its fiery chutneys, biryanis, and traditional meals served on banana leaves.

Handicrafts & Art

Discover beautiful handloom textiles like Pochampally and Kalamkari, alongside exquisite Kondapalli toys and vibrant paintings representing Andhra’s artistic traditions.
