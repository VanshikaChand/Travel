Staying Options in Andhra Pradesh

Hotels

Andhra Pradesh offers a range of hotels from luxury 5-star resorts to comfortable mid-range hotels in major cities like Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, and Tirupati.


The Gateway Hotel, Visakhapatnam

Luxury hotel with sea views, pool, spa, and multiple dining options.


Fortune Murali Park, Vijayawada

Comfortable rooms, great location, and modern amenities.


Marasa Sarovar Premiere, Tirupati

Elegant stay near the famous Tirupati temple with excellent service.

Guest Houses

Guest houses offer budget-friendly stays with basic facilities and a homely atmosphere, suitable for travelers looking for affordable and cozy accommodation.


Sai Guest House, Visakhapatnam

Clean rooms and friendly hosts, located close to the beach.


Sri Krishna Guest House, Vijayawada

Affordable rooms with essential amenities near city center.


Lakshmi Guest House, Tirupati

Simple accommodation with easy access to the temple.

Homestays

Experience authentic local culture by staying with host families in homestays available in rural areas and tourist spots.


Rural Homestay, Araku Valley

Stay amidst nature with traditional food and warm hospitality.


Village Life Homestay, Srikakulam

Immerse yourself in local culture and lifestyle.


Green Fields Homestay, Vizianagaram

Peaceful retreat with organic meals and farm activities.

Budget Lodges

For budget-conscious travelers, lodges provide basic accommodation at very affordable prices across the state.


Budget Lodge, Visakhapatnam

Simple rooms, clean environment, perfect for short stays.


City Lodge, Vijayawada

Affordable lodging near the bus station.


Travelers Lodge, Tirupati

Basic facilities with easy access to pilgrimage sites.
