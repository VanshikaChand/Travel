Welcome to Meghalaya



The Abode of Clouds Awaits You



Living Root Bridges

Marvel at the ingenious natural bridges grown from rubber tree roots — a unique eco-friendly marvel of Meghalaya.

Cherrapunji & Mawsynram

Visit the wettest places on Earth, home to spectacular waterfalls like Nohkalikai and breathtaking misty landscapes.

Tribal Culture & Festivals

Experience the vibrant Khasi, Jaintia, and Garo tribal traditions through festivals like Wangala and Nongkrem, full of music and dance.

Caves & Nature

Explore the sprawling limestone caves like Siju and Mawmluh, and enjoy Meghalaya’s rich biodiversity in its dense forests.

Traditional Handicrafts

Discover beautifully woven shawls, bamboo crafts, and pottery that reflect the artistry of Meghalaya’s tribes.

Local Cuisine

Taste traditional dishes like Jadoh (rice with meat), Dohneiiong (pork curry), and Tungrymbai (fermented soybeans).
