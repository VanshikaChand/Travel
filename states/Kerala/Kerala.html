Welcome to Kerala



God's Own Country of Backwaters and Bliss



Enchanting Backwaters

Explore the serene backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom on traditional houseboats, gliding through a network of lagoons, lakes, and canals surrounded by lush greenery.

Lush Hill Stations

Visit the cool hill stations of Munnar and Wayanad, with sprawling tea gardens, misty mountains, and diverse wildlife sanctuaries for nature lovers and trekkers.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Experience Kerala’s classical art forms like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam. Attend vibrant temple festivals showcasing the state’s rich traditions and folklore.

Ayurvedic Wellness

Kerala is the birthplace of Ayurveda. Rejuvenate your mind and body with authentic Ayurvedic treatments, massages, and wellness retreats amidst tranquil surroundings.

Delicious Cuisine

Indulge in Kerala’s flavorful cuisine featuring fresh seafood, coconut-based curries, and traditional Sadya meals served on banana leaves during festivals.

Wildlife & Nature

Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary for elephant sightings, bird watching, and nature walks through dense forests and picturesque landscapes.
